🌞 Simple Story: "Max and the Island"

It was summer. I went to the beach with my cousin Anna and our dog, Max. We saw a small boat...

On the island, we saw a funny sign. It said, "Beware of monkeys!" We laughed. Then three monkeys jumped from the trees!

Anna had fruit. We gave the monkeys bananas. Later, we found a cave filled with shiny rocks. It looked like treasure!

The sky turned dark and the wind blew hard. Max barked and helped us go home. It was exciting!
